To meet various compliance requirements, you may want to self-host Gsoc2 instead of using [Gsoc2 Cloud](https://app.gsoc2.com/).
Self-hosted Gsoc2 allows you to maintain your sensitive information within your own infrastructure and network, ensuring complete control over your data.
